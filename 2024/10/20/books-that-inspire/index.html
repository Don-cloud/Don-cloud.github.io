Reading List¶

In this blog, I share a carefully curated list of books, tech tools and other resources that have significantly impacted my growth as a technology professional. Whether you’re looking to enhance your technical skills, explore cloud computing, or stay upto-date with cutting-edge tools, these recommendations offer both practical knowledge and inspiration. Ideal for anyone aiming to deepen their expertise, boost productivity, and ignite their passion for innovation

  • The Phoenix Project: By Gene Kim, Kevin Behr and George Spafford. A Novel about IT DevOps transformation of an organization
  • Kubernetes in Action by Marko Luska. a comprehensive guide to effectively developing and running applications in a Kubernetes environment
  • The Five Dysfunctions of a Team By Patrick M. Lencioni A leadership fable of how to build effective teams.
  • Let Us C By Yashavant kanetkar. An Authentic guide to C programming language
  • Made to Stick by Chip Heath, Dan Heath. Why Some Ideas Survive and Others Die
